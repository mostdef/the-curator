# Ideas for Further Development

## Now Watching Widget
- Auto-suggest "watch next" from watchlist after a decision
- "Cinematic mode" — full-screen takeover version of the widget for a more immersive pre-screening feel
- Re-watch tracking — detect if a movie is already in collection and offer "re-evaluate" instead of categorize
- Notes field — quick one-liner reaction after watching
- Star/score rating in addition to list categorization
- Watch history log — track what was watched and when (watchedAt timestamps)
- Social: share "currently watching" state (future multi-user)

## Recommendation Engine
- Auto-trigger a new recommendation after logging a watched film
- Incorporate watching frequency/velocity into taste profile
- "Watch tonight" suggestions based on mood/time available (filter by runtime)

## General
- PWA / mobile support for logging on the couch
- Push notification reminder: "You added X to watchlist 2 weeks ago"
- Streaming availability check (JustWatch API integration)
- Import from Letterboxd CSV
